How the math works (transparent by design):
  • Platform fees (commission, fixed, eKart shipping, collection) all depend on the selling price, so the app solves for the price that delivers your target profit after every fee and cost — not a rough add-on.
  • Returns: for the return % you set, each returned unit is provisioned with lost forward shipping + reverse shipping + damage loss (WSP) + re-pack/labour for resold units.
  • GST: the retail price is shown GST-inclusive. For a registered seller claiming ITC, product GST is pass-through and the 18% GST on fees is reclaimed; switch ITC off for a conservative price.
  • Market price is an estimate from your spread settings; add competitor prices for a real min/max/average.

Disclaimer: Fee defaults reflect Flipkart's Nov-2025 rate revision (Gold tier) as published in mid-2026 and are indicative. Actual commission, fixed fee and eKart rates vary by seller tier, category, sub-category and your specific rate card. Always confirm in Flipkart Seller Hub → Reports → My Commission Structure and overwrite the rate card here. Not affiliated with Flipkart / Shopsy.
